Keep It Short

As par of our commitment to nurtuting talent, we are organizing an Online Movie Talent Contest-Keep it Short to discover and celebrate the next generation of storytellers, a competitive reality show for up-coming filmmakers to showcase their filmmaking talents through their short films 3-4 minutes, and compete with other aspiring filmmakers to win prize money, and where "THE AUDIENCE" will be the judges.

This contest is a unique oppoertunity for the students of Film and Animation Institures to showcase their creativity, hone their filmmaking skills, and gain recognition on a larger and dedicated platform.
